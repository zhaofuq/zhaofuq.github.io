I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science from ShanghaiTech University, advised by Prof. Jingyi Yu. I am currently the President and Co-founder of Crysta AI, a startup focused on AI Generative 3D full-color Printing technologies.

My research interests span several key areas, including 3D Reconstruction & Rendering based on NeRF/3DGS, Camera Controlled Video Generation and 3D Generative AI. I have a great passion for Neural Rendering, 3D Reconstruction and Generation, aiming to bridge the gap between academic research and industrial applications.
