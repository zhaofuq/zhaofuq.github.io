ShanghaiTech University | NeuDim Digital Technology Inc.

I am a Ph.D. candidate at ShanghaiTech University, advised by Prof. Jingyi Yu. I am also the CEO and Founder of NeuDim Digital Technology Inc., a startup focused on neural 3D/4D reconstruction technologies.

My research interests spans several key areas, including 3D Reconstruction & Rendering based on NeRF/3DGS, and 3D Generative AI. I have a great passion for Neural Rendering, 3D Reconstruction and Generation, aiming to bridge the gap between academic research and industrial applications.
